Who I Am

Hello! My name is Ramit Pariyar, and I am a Computer Engineering Student currently based in Surkhet, originally from Kushe Rural Municipality, Jajarkot. I have a strong passion for technology, especially in the field of web development and software systems.

From the beginning of my academic journey, I have been deeply interested in how systems work and how technology can be used to solve real-life problems. Over time, I have developed practical skills in designing and building web-based applications that are both functional and user-friendly.

I enjoy creating clean, modern, and responsive websites that provide a smooth user experience. I am always eager to learn new technologies, improve my coding skills, and take on new challenges that help me grow as a developer.

My goal is to become a highly skilled professional developer and contribute to impactful projects that make a difference in people's lives. I believe in continuous learning, hard work, and creativity, which drive me forward in my journey.
